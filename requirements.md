## Grading

There's a maximum score: 100 points.

- A+ 100% to 94.0%
- A < 94.0% to 90.0%
- A- < 90.0% to 87.0%
- B+ < 87.0% to 84.0%
- B < 84.0% to 80.0%
- B- < 80.0% to 77.0%
- C+ < 77.0% to 74.0%
- C < 74.0% to 70.0%
- C- < 70.0% to 67.0%
- D+ < 67.0% to 64.0%
- D < 64.0% to 61.0%
- F < 61.0% to 0.0%
